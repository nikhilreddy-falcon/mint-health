## üé≠ Demo Best Practices

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Start with the problem** - "You're drowning in spreadsheets..."
- **Use real numbers** - Don't say "some data", say "$32.8M in AR"
- **Tell a story** - Connect dashboards to business decisions
- **Pause for questions** - Let them absorb each section
- **Point and click slowly** - Give them time to see
- **Highlight actionability** - "You can immediately prioritize these 15 invoices..."

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't rush through dashboards
- Don't use technical jargon
- Don't skip loading times (just talk through them)
- Don't apologize for demo data ("In your case...")
- Don't demo features you don't understand
- Don't ignore errors (refresh if needed)
